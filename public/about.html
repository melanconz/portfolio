ABOUT

Programming and application development provides a seemingly endless stream of potential knowledge that I have been soaking myself in for nearly two years.

I majored in English with a concentration in writing at Lousiana State University because I loved to create. I discovered and minored in Philosophy there because I love to logically solve problems, and I didn't worry about the amount of course work this would provide me because I am diligent, and I love to learn.

It wasn't until, five year after graduating and becoming a teacher, that I discovered programming, which perfectly combines those three passions: creating, problem solving, and learning. Once I decided to change careers, I quickly began to absorb knowledge and build skills.

At Bloc, I learned Ruby and Ruby on Rails, Data Seeding, Test-Driven Development, Authentication, Authorization, Building and Using APIs, AJAX, and JSON to build out scalable and perfomant backends, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, AngularJS to build user interfaces that run smoothly, intuitively, and beauitifully.

At IBM, I took advantage of classes in Java and Swift for Android and iOS Development and Bluemix, design patterns, algorithms, security which allowed me to grow as a developer. I, also, began my first project building out the Angular front end for an application that used Watson technology to quickly and efficiently find caparables for companies being audited. During which time, I gained experience in UI Grid, Angular Material, Agile Development and more.

At Trinity Integrated Solutions, I worked on multiple applications at a time. Some applications were built in QuickBase, which was created to limit the need for code, and therefore, required that I focus on building JavaScript, jQuery, CSS, and HTML skills. Other applications were mobile based, built using Node.JS, Express.JS, AngularJS, Ionic, Cordova as well as Jade, Sass, Angular Material, and other tools.

Currently, I am working part-time, at night and on weekends, for Alzcare Labs a company that is working build technology that will serve as an infrastructure for dementia-friendly communities. I have built their top level website (alzcarelabs.com) and will be leading the devlopment of their React Native iOS application that connects to their FindMe wearable IoT device.

Because this is not a full time position, I am hoping to find a company whose needs fits my skill set.
